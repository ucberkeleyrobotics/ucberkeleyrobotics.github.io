Automation Lab

Contact: Professor Ken Goldberg - goldberg@berkeley.edu

Description:

The Berkeley Laboratory for Automation Science and Engineering investigates four primary areas in robotics: cloud robotics, robotic surgery, automated manufacturing, and new media art forms. See more at their website: automation.berkeley.edu.
