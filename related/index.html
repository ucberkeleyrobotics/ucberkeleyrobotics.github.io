Related Groups

As you may well know, UC Berkeley is a very exciting place for roboticists! There is an immense amount of cutting-edge robotics research here that constantly advances the broad and diverse fields that robotics spans. From self-driving cars to robotic surgery, intelligent laundry-folding robots to machines that mimic animal motions, the projects that Berkeley minds work on every day exceed many roboticists' wildest dreams.

We are just one tiny part of this expansive and amazing community of roboticists at UC Berkeley, so we want to recognize some other incredible groups that we admire.
