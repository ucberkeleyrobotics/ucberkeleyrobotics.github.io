How can I contribute?

There are a ton of ways that you can help us out! Here are some of the things that you can assist with.

As a member of our Mechanical Team, you will assist with things like the suspension, controls, and motors of the robot

On our Electrical Team, you will do things like design the circuit of the robot and ensure that electrical connections are soldered properly.

Our Strategy Team is also invaluable to the quality of our robot, as the team does things like telemetry, system monitoring, programming, and other tasks to ensure our robot runs as efficiently as possible.

Lastly, our Operations Team handles everything from corporate sponsorships to the team's finances. It ensures that the team can continue being well-funded for years to come.


Mission Statement

The mission of the Cal Robotics Team is to provide a community that openly offers equipment, project funding, and inspiration to UC Berkeley students interested in robotics or in the disciplines that contribute to the field of robotics. We strive to be an organization in which students can explore their own ideas, connect with peers who hold similar interests, build their organizational and communication skills, and hone their abilities to design and build complex machines.
