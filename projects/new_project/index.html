Starting a New Project

Want to run a project of your own? Need money to purchase materials? Submit a project proposal and receive Robotics Team support!

To submit a proposal for a new project, follow the steps below:

  1. Come up with a cool project idea!
  2. Write a (1-2 page) project proposal with the following sections:
    • A list of people currently committed to the project idea, and the number of people you would like to have working on the project
    • Short summary and sketch(es) of the project idea
    • Projected timeline of the project, including start date, when materials will be purchased, the dates of any major intermediate design or build stages, and project end date
    • Outline of the finances for the project, including what needs to be purchased
  3. Submit the project proposal to the executive committee via email.
  4. An officer will contact you shortly after your submission. If your project proposal is not currently feasible, they will inform you of this. If feasible, one or multiple officers will meet with you to discuss how financial transactions will work.
  5. An officer will be assigned to your project to make sure it runs on schedule. They will check that you are reaching your major deadlines. The officers may require that you adjust your deadlines or finances according to the progression of the project.
  6. Begin your project!
