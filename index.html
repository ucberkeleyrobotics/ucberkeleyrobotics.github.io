Welcome to the Robotics at Berkeley website!

We are a student group at UC Berkeley that lowers the barriers to being involved in robotics at Cal by doing the following things:

&emsp 1. Supporting other students' robotics projects

2. Increasing awareness and accessibility of all robotics-related groups

3. Organizing social and intellectual events focused on robotics
