Welcome to the Robotics at Berkeley website!

We are a student group at UC Berkeley that lowers the barriers to being involved in robotics at Cal by doing the following things:

  • Supporting other students' robotics projects by helping them get the money, space, tools, and advising they need.
  • Increasing awareness and accessibility of all robotics-related groups on campus by collecting and distributing information about different robotics organizations and events in and around UC Berkeley.
  • Organizes social and intellectual events focused on robotics and related technologies.

    • We encourage you to explore our website! Especially be sure to check out our Also at Cal page if you want to discover all the different groups involved in robotics at UC Berkeley.
